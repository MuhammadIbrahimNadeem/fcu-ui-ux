# 💀 DESIGN PROMPT — “JAHANNUM LOGIN PORTAL 3000”

Create a hilariously cursed Pakistani-themed login experience with the worst UI/UX imaginable while still technically functional. The design should feel like a mix of:

* a broken NADRA website,
* a shady university portal,
* 2009 Facebook chain messages,
* expired Easypaisa app UI,
* and a government captcha designed by sleep-deprived interns.

The entire experience should emotionally damage the user.

## 🎨 OVERALL VISUAL STYLE

* Extremely chaotic layout
* Neon green + bright red + dark purple gradients fighting each other
* Comic Sans mixed with Times New Roman and random Arabic fonts
* Buttons misaligned by 2 pixels intentionally
* Everything slightly stretched
* Windows XP vibes mixed with TikTok aesthetics
* Random sparkles and fire GIFs
* Pakistani meme energy everywhere
* Tiny unreadable labels
* Popup overload
* Fake loading bars
* Cursor trails
* Shaky animations
* Floating Urdu motivational quotes
* “FREE MBs CLICK HERE” banner ads

Add:

* distorted emojis
* spinning goats
* low-resolution pigeons
* dancing chai cups
* loud warning icons

  * empty chai cups

  * 1 Urdu proverb
  * 1 punctuation mark
  * your blood group
  * your enemy’s nickname
* Must NOT contain:

  * vowels
  * happiness

  * hope

### Timer
